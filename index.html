About the Project

The project began with an initial idea of building a car simsulator. We wanted to make a VR-linked RC car driving game that teaches real-world driving skills in a safe, interactive way. Players wear a VR headset to experience lifelike roads while controlling an RC car on a miniature track, completing levels, challenges, and missions that simulate real driving situations.
